# Enhance Hooks

Use this module when the user asks to improve the opening, retention, rhythm, suspense, transitions, or "why keep watching" of a short-form video.

## Big Hook

The big hook must quickly establish at least one of:

- What this video is about.
- Why it is worth watching.
- What may happen next.
- What feeling, information, or change the viewer will gain.

## Big Hook Types

- 情绪钩子: Start from a recognizable feeling, such as tiredness, calm, envy, relief, confusion, or being stuck.
- 问题钩子: Ask the exact question the viewer already has or should suddenly care about.
- 画面钩子: Open with a concrete image that makes the viewer stop.
- 悬念钩子: Show an incomplete action, unresolved outcome, or delayed explanation.
- 结果钩子: Show the final change first, then invite the viewer to see how it happened.
- 观点钩子: State a fresh, slightly surprising observation without exaggerating.

## Opening Patterns

Use or adapt these patterns:

- "我本来以为____，直到____。"
- "这件小事让我突然意识到____。"
- "如果你也____，可以先看完这个过程。"
- "我用____，把____变成了____。"
- "真正让这条内容成立的，不是____，而是____。"
- "今天这个画面看起来很普通，但它最后落在了____。"
- "为什么____之后，反而更____？"

## Small Hooks

Small hooks connect chapters and create light expectation. They can be:

- A question.
- An unfinished action.
- A light suspense.
- An emotional shift.
- A small contrast.
- A line with aftertaste.
- A new observation.
- A stage turn.

## Small Hook Patterns

- "但真正的变化，是从这里开始的。"
- "我以为这样就结束了，结果____。"
- "做到这一步，我才发现____。"
- "问题不是____，而是____。"
- "接下来这个细节，决定了后面的感觉。"
- "这个动作很小，但它让____变了。"

## Hook Quality Check

A hook is weak if it only announces the topic. Strengthen it by adding viewer benefit, visible contrast, emotional tension, an unfinished action, or a concrete result.

A hook is too loud if it promises drama the video cannot deliver. Keep the hook aligned with the actual emotional scale of the content.
